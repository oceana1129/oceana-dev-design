My journey.

I worked as an artist when the pandemic hit. What started with art commissions eventually grew into running a merchandise line featuring my own artwork. While managing my business, I looked for ways to streamline my workflow, which led me to programming.

I started by building a simple frontend website and creating scripts to automate business tasks. Before long, I was fully immersed in software development and decided to transition from art to technology. Today, I’m finishing my Computer Science degree while freelancing, helping clients prototype applications and build websites.

I’m currently working as a freelance developer.

While completing my Computer Science degree, I help clients prototype applications and build websites for their brands. My work includes UX/UI design and full stack development.

I run a small business.

I sell my artwork online and at local conventions. Most of my creations are cat themed or character design work.

I’ve been drawing since childhood, inspired by anime, video games, and fantasy. I create character designs, make 3d models, and get commissioned to make art for others. I’m always exploring new ways to grow as an artist!
